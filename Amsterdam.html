Tulip Gardens also known as Keukenhof, is the best day out among the flowers! Over 7 million bulbs will bloom this spring, with a total of 800 varieties of tulips. A unique, unforgettable experience! Besides the spacious 32 hectares of flowers you can enjoy the spectacular flower shows, surprising inspirational gardens, unique art and wonderful events. A great day out for the whole family.

Canal Belt Tour is criss-crossed by bridges, 165 canals encircle the city of Amsterdam and keep the sea at bay. The waterways provide an attractive border to the arty locales of the Museum Quarter. Within the pockets of land that their eclectic network creates, you can find shops, galleries and authentic cafés. Lined by shady trees and funky houseboats. As you wander up to this area, you'll find the tall spire of the Westerkerk and the modest Anne Frank Huis.

Van Gogh Museum holds 200 paintings and 500 drawings produced by the troubled genius, as well as Japanese paints and works by his one-time collaborator Gauguin. It is housed in a Rietveld building, enlarged with a newer wing by Japanese architect Kisho Kurokawa. On the gorund floor, there is also an interactive exhibit for visitors to leave their own mark in the museum!

Restaurant Blauw is an Indonesian spot renowned for its rijsttafel, which is the thing to order. As a former dutch colony, Indonesia restaurants are commonplace among the streets of Amsterdam boasting traditional Asian tastes and fragrances. Blauw is one a business casual type restaurant for those wanting a fancier night out. Arrive hungry!

The Fat Dog For hot dogs, look no further than the Fat Dog, Amsterdam’s first-ever hot dog joint, opened by acclaimed restaurateur Ron Blaauw in 2014. Order an all-pork frank with sauerkraut, mustard, and onion marmalade (called “Gangs of New York”) or go for the chicken hot dog with satay sauce (spiced coconut flakes). Innovation doesn’t stop there: The lamb dog comes with baba ganoush, and there’s also a veggie dog.

Conservatorium Brasserie and Lounge With its immense floor-to-ceiling windows and glass ceiling, this is hands-down the most impressive lobby-cum-all-day-dining-room in Amsterdam — an essential part of the total experience at this cosmopolitan hotel. Enjoy drinks and snacks in the lounge area or go to the brasserie for lunch or dinner. Standout dishes include lobster au gratin, steak, and apple crumble.
