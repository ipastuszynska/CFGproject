Wine Tasting here brings exposure to elegant whites, aromatic rosés or powerful reds...Provence’s wines are full of surprises. How about learning more about them? Leaving from Aix-en-Provence, set off to meet our region’s winegrowers who will explain everything you need to know about ‘terroirs’, local grape varieties and winemaking methods.

Triathlon The Territory of Pays d'Aix is known as a training paradise to many cyclists and triathletes. Athletes embark on a one-loop, 2 km swim that takes place in the waters of Lake Peyrolles. During the 90 km bike course, athletes will pass through Pays d'Aix and eight surrounding towns, which allow for spectator support. A bike course and a three-loop, 21 km run course brings athletes through city and forest roads through La Torse Park.

Hiking is a great way to breathe in the scrubland’s scents, enjoy the warm sun, listen to the sounds of nature, marvel at the powerful landscapes and views of Provence... this is what these 11 walks and hiking tours in the heart of Aix Region are all about. The marked trails of about 10 to 12 km long, lead you on the paths of history, amidst vineyards and charming villages in the Mediterranean mountains and forests, to discover the treasures of our land.

Le Jardin d'Amalula has a lovely hidden garden in the back. There is an option to order the special which comes with a dessert and tea or coffee. This restaurant offers traditional French foods and lovely wine pairings aas well! Come here and enjoy the friendly service, delicious and fresh food, and relaxing outdoor seating.

L'Epicurien Restauran is a fantastic fine-dining option in this charming town. Get dressed up enjoy the excellent staff and service in this intimate space. This stunning Michelin starred restaurant also features a quaint, rustic terrace. Traditional French food featuring gastronomic creations by the talented kitchen.

Pâtisserie Weibel is a bit of a different option for the customers. This is a beautiful dessert bar in the heart of the town! The pâtisserie is well decorated and caters also caters to vegetarian and vegan consumers. And once again, in true French fashion, there is also al fresco seating for those wanting to enjoy the nice weather in Spring and Summer.
